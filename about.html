Who We Are

Built for transportation, not just Salesforce

CloudCrest was founded with a singular focus — helping transportation companies get the most out of Salesforce. Not as a side practice. As our entire reason for existing.

Our Story

We saw the gap — and filled it

Transportation companies were being sold generic Salesforce implementations by consultants who didn't understand the industry. The result was systems that technically worked but didn't reflect how freight actually moves — how brokers manage carriers, how fleets track assets, how dispatch and sales need to talk to each other.

CloudCrest was founded to fix that. We brought together people with deep roots in both Salesforce and transportation — and made a deliberate choice to stay focused. We don't serve every industry. We serve yours.

That focus makes us faster, more accurate, and more useful than a generalist consultant trying to learn your business on your dime.

Our Values

How we work

These aren't wall posters. They're the principles that show up in every client engagement, every delivery, and every conversation.

Industry Focus

We only serve transportation. That means no learning curve, no generic playbooks, and no time wasted figuring out how your business works. We already know.

Partnership Over Projects

We don't disappear after go-live. Our best client relationships are the long ones — where we're embedded in your growth, not just a line item on a past invoice.

Honest Expertise

We tell you what you need to hear, not what you want to hear. If something won't work, we say so. If there's a better path, we'll find it — even if it's more work for us.

Respect for Your Time

Transportation doesn't stop. We work around your operation, not the other way around. Fast responses, clear timelines, and no project theater.

Built to Last

We build systems designed to grow with you — not ones that require a full rebuild every two years. Sustainable architecture, clean code, and documentation that actually helps.

Show Up

We believe in being present — literally. On-site when it matters, available when you need us, and genuinely invested in your outcomes. Not just available on a ticket system.
